Seongkyu Choi

I am an M.S. student at Gachon University, South Korea, conducting research in 2D/3D computer vision for autonomous driving and robotics.

My research focuses on visual perception in complex environments, including off-road semantic segmentation, BEV-based scene understanding, multi-task perception, and robust 3D spatial representation.

I am particularly interested in developing efficient and reliable perception models that can operate under ambiguous boundaries, noisy supervision, and challenging real-world conditions for intelligent vehicles, mobile robots, and autonomous systems.
